We are working on adding parking service time details for all cities in Sweden.
Please wait for the app updates or follow us on twitter, instagram to get news instantly.

  • Green line means parking is allowed right now with a valid residence (boende­parkerings­tillstånd och biljett) permit; otherwise allowed for a fee.
  • Blue line means parking is allowed for a fee right now.
  • Amber line means parking will be prohibited later in the day.
  • Red line means no parking is allowed right now.

We use officially available parking service data. Unfortunately, even the official one is not 100% accurate.
Please always double-check road signs when parking your car and report your findings to us. We will update the data in the next release of the App.

The current version of ParkIN shows the district/area defined in settings. We believe that even with this limitation ParkIN can be useful for you and will help to avoid fines near your home location. Later there will be an option to access parking data of the entire city/region or all supported cities in Sweden.

P.S. There is no such limitation in the city of Solna as the first city supported by ParkIN.
